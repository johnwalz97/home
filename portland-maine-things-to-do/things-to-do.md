# Things To Do in Portland, Maine

**Created:** 2026-06-19

A curated checklist distilled from the deep-research guide. Full details, tips, and sources are in **`presentation.md`** (written) and **`presentation.html`** (slide deck).

## 🦞 Eat
- [ ] Brown-butter lobster roll + oysters at **Eventide Oyster Co.** (go at opening)
- [ ] Duck-fat fries at **Duckfat** (or the quicker Friteshack window)
- [ ] Dinner at **Fore Street** (reserve well ahead)
- [ ] Potato donut at **The Holy Donut** (morning, before flavors sell out)
- [ ] Morning bun at **Standard Baking Co.**
- [ ] $1 oyster happy hour at **Jacqueline**
- [ ] Saturday **Portland Farmers' Market** at Deering Oaks Park

## 🍺 Drink
- [ ] **Industrial Way** brewery crawl — Allagash, Foundation, Battery Steele
- [ ] **East Bayside** crawl — Rising Tide, Goodfire, Lone Pine
- [ ] Bissell Brothers "The Substance" at Thompson's Point
- [ ] Cocktails at **Blyth & Burrows** or **Portland Hunt & Alpine Club**
- [ ] Tour **New England Distilling** (first since Prohibition)

## 💡 Lighthouses
- [ ] **Portland Head Light** & Fort Williams Park (go early)
- [ ] **Bug Light** at sunset
- [ ] Walk the breakwater to **Spring Point Ledge Light**
- [ ] **Two Lights State Park** + the Lobster Shack

## ⛴️ On the water
- [ ] **Casco Bay Lines** Mailboat Run
- [ ] Bike the loop on **Peaks Island**
- [ ] Sea-kayak to **Fort Gorges** with Portland Paddle
- [ ] Lighthouse cruise or whale watch

## 🌳 Outdoors & town
- [ ] Sunrise walk on the **Eastern Promenade**
- [ ] Back Cove loop
- [ ] Beach day — Crescent / Higgins / Old Orchard Beach

## 🏛️ Culture & history
- [ ] **Portland Museum of Art** (free Friday evenings)
- [ ] Climb the **Portland Observatory**
- [ ] **Victoria Mansion** (reserve ahead)
- [ ] Wadsworth-Longfellow House
- [ ] Walk the **Portland Freedom Trail**
- [ ] Shop Maine-made goods on **Exchange Street**

## 🚗 Day trips
- [ ] **Freeport / L.L. Bean** flagship (open 24/7)
- [ ] **Old Orchard Beach** boardwalk
- [ ] **Kennebunkport**
- [ ] (Overnight) **Acadia National Park** — ~3.5 hrs each way

---
*Tip: most museums, lighthouse interiors, and historic houses are seasonal (≈May/June–October). Confirm 2026 hours and prices on official sites before you go.*
